What is Custom AI CRM?

Custom AI CRM is an intelligent customer relationship management system designed from the ground up for real estate professionals. Unlike generic CRMs that require extensive customization, our platform understands the unique workflows, terminology, and challenges of real estate from day one.

Powered by AI, the system automatically categorizes leads, predicts deal probability, suggests next actions, and automates repetitive tasks. Every interaction is tracked, analyzed, and used to provide actionable insights that help you close more deals faster.

How Custom AI CRM Works

1

Lead Capture

Leads automatically flow into the CRM from all sources: website, ads, referrals, open houses, and third-party platforms.

2

AI Classification

AI analyzes lead data, assigns scores, categorizes by type, and routes to the appropriate agent or team member.

3

Automated Workflows

Trigger sequences for follow-up emails, SMS, tasks, and reminders based on lead behavior and pipeline stage.

4

Deal Management

Track progress through each pipeline stage with automated status updates, document management, and collaboration tools.
