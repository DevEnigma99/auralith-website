What is Transaction Coordination?

Transaction Coordination is an intelligent system that manages every aspect of your real estate transactions from contract to close. Our AI automates document collection, tracks deadlines, ensures compliance, and coordinates with all parties to keep deals moving forward smoothly.

The platform eliminates the stress of transaction management by automatically generating checklists, sending reminders, validating documents, and flagging potential issues before they become problems. Every transaction is tracked in real-time with complete visibility for agents, clients, and stakeholders.

Why It Matters

Automated Checklists

AI generates transaction-specific checklists with all required tasks, documents, and deadlines based on deal type and location.

Deadline Tracking

Never miss a critical date. Automated reminders keep everyone on track for inspections, contingencies, and closing dates.

Document Management

Centralized storage for all transaction documents with version control, e-signatures, and secure sharing.

Compliance Assurance

AI validates documents against state and local requirements to ensure regulatory compliance and reduce liability.

Party Coordination

Automated communication with buyers, sellers, lenders, title companies, and inspectors keeps everyone informed.

Issue Detection

AI identifies potential problems like missing signatures, expired documents, or conflicting terms before they derail the deal.
